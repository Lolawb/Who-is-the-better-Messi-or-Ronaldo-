During in games, Lionel Messi also has a very strange tactic. If you watch him play in a game, you may notice that he isn't running as much as you would expect. Messi himself hasn't commented on his strategy of walking, but one of his former coaches has given an explanation. "He's not running, but he is always watching what happens," Pep Guardiola, Messi's former Barcelona coach, said in the "This Is Football" documentary, per The Athletic.
